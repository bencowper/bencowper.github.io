Ben Cowper


In 2007 I founded a freelance video production company. Since then I have filmed and edited over 100 projects for a wide variety of commercial and independent clients including: Yale University, Cambridge University Press, Guilford Art Center, Shoreline Actors Collective, Hampshire College, Mount Holyoke College, ServiceNet, Northampton Chamber of Commerce, Beehive Sewing, and the Global Fibrosis Foundation.

I received a Bachelor of Arts in Film and Video Production from Hampshire College in 2016. There I worked on various independent film productions and am proficient in the following areas:

Cinematography Canon 5DmkIII (Magic Lantern), Canon C100, Blackmagic, GoPro, 16mm Bolex, Super 8, Glidecam

Editing Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve

Audio Mixing Zoom, Tascam, ProTools, Logic, Adobe Audition 5.1 surround and stereo

Photography Digital, 35mm, medium format
