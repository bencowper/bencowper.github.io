Ben Cowper

cowpervideos@gmail.com


I began my work in film production over 10 years ago, and in 2016 received a Bachelor of Arts in Cinematography and Editing from Hampshire College. As a freelance videographer I have filmed and edited over 100 videos for a wide variety of commercial and independent clients including: Yale University, Hampshire College, Mount Holyoke College, PBS American Masters, Cambridge University Press, ServiceNet, Northampton Chamber of Commerce, Beehive Sewing, and the Global Fibrosis Foundation.

I am proficient in the following areas of production:

Cinematography: Canon 5DmkIII (Magic Lantern), Canon C100, Blackmagic, GoPro, 16mm Bolex, Super 8, Glidecam, still photography (digital, 35mm, medium, large format)

Editing: Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve

Audio: 5.1 surround and stereo recording/mixing: Zoom, Tascam, ProTools, Audition, Logic

Lighting Design: Film, Television, and Theatre: Arri, Lowel, Kino, ETC
